#Reverse Alarm Clock

This is an "alarm clock" which warns you ahead of time that an alarm is coming, as well as nagging you after the time of the alarm.


# Credits

It was developed by @anall for @jeshyr, who had trouble remembering to go to sleep despite her best efforts. It really helps, too!
